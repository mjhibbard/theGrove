OUR PASTORS

Pastor Micahn Carter has been the Lead Pastor of Together Church for 13 years and is on a mission to Make It Impossible for People to Not Know Jesus. Together Church began with 50 people in roughest part of the city and is now a few thousand people in 3 locations. He is determined to use any influence he has been given to build people.

Pastor Micahn is known for his straight forward and humorous communication style. He and his wife April have been married for 18 years and have three sons: Meeks, Alijah and Dewey.

Our Code

OUR TOGETHER CHURCH CODE IS ADAPTED FROM LUKE 15
Click on each one to reveal more about what it means to us
IT'S ALL ABOUT HIM

WE LOVE HIM AND WHAT HE LOVES (VS. 7).

THIS IS A SEARCH AND RESCUE MISSION

WE WILL NEVER ALLOW THIS LIFEBOAT TO BECOME A YACHT CLUB. (VS. 8)

LEAVE THE 99 EVERYTIME

WE WILL ALWAYS PUT MORE EFFORT INTO REACHING THE LOST THAN KEEPING THE FOUND. (VS. 4)

FATTEN THE COW

WE WILL GIVE OUR RESOURCES CHEERFULLY IN PREPARATION FOR THE PARTY. (VS. 23)

EVERYONE IS INVITED

WE WILL EMBRACE DIVERSITY WHILE MAINTAINING UNITY. (VS. 22-24)

CELEBRATION IS NOT AN OPTION

WE WILL CELEBRATE EVERY STORY AND EVERY PERSON, IT ALL MATTERS. (VS. 32)

LET THE PARTY BEGIN

WE WILL SPARE NO EXPENSE. THE JUST1MORE IS WORTH IT. (VS. 23-24)

IT'S NOT ABOUT ME

WE WILL NOT TAKE THIS FOR GRANTED. WE ARE GRATEFUL FOR ALL HE’S DONE AND ALL HE PLANS TO DO. (VS. 29-31)

UNITY IS GREATER THAN LOYALTY

WE WILL STICK TOGETHER. (VS. 28)
